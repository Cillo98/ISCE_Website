Professors, PhD, master and bachelor students at XJTLU. We hope, that thereby students from different departments (physics, finance, marketing, IT, etc.) working on the same industry can find each other and cooperate. Moreover, we hope, that students still looking for a dissertation topic can feel inspired by others current work and join the respective workgroup. Finally, we hope to create a XJTLU community focused on research and career exploration.

Please click on the field tot he left to the left, to have a look at the already established workgroups. in case you are interested to join one of them, please contact the workgroup initiator. In case you are interested to set up and advertise your own workgroup at XJTLU, please contact the ISCE team WeChat contact at the header.
